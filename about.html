TRAVEL

I've explored around: New Zealand, Peru, Japan and the islands of Java and Bali in Indonesia. I prefer nature to cities and absolutely love seeing the unique landscapes, flora and fauna that the environments have to offer.

ART

I play around with watercolours and digital work in Inkscape. Surrealism art is my favourite style favouring the styles of: Zdzisław Beksiński, Salvador Dalí and Dean Bernardini.

BOOKS

I read every single day. I lean towards philosophy - particularly on the subject of the mind, consciousness, free will and the subjective experience of other beings. However a good book on science, biographies, journalism and my fair share of fiction is found among my collection.

GARDEN

I gain immesnse satisfaction from watching plants grow. I've got a nice collection of cacti, succulents, tasty fruit plants and pretty trees. My favourite of which is my Brugmansia tree for its incredible flowers.

COMPUTERS

Experimenting with available software such as: Photoshop, GIMP, Inkscape, Cubase and ProTools. I play around with some programming languages, mainly: HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP and Python.
