INSTRUCTIONS

With this metronome you can sequence your own customized BPM and Time Signature changes to loop through.

BPM - This sets your Beats Per Minute

Next Accent - This sets how long before the next accented beat. For Example if you wanted a 7/8 count with the 1, 4 and 6 beats accented then you would add a 3 and two 2's to the sequence. (The first beat is always accented, the number you select in this field is how many beats will be counted out before the next accent.)

Count Beats - Select whether the BPM is counting 1/4 notes or 1/8 notes.

Repeat - This quickly sets multiple repeats. For Example if you wanted three counts of 4/4 and one count of 3/4 to loop you would set:
[Next Accent: 4] [Count Beats: 1/4] [Repeats: 3] "Add To Sequence" (adding 3x bars of 4/4)
THEN
[Next Accent: 3] [Count Beats: 1/4] [Repeats: 1] "Add To Sequence" (adding 1x bar of 3/4). The sequence loops until you stop it.

*Tip: The START button will always jump you to the SEQUENCE page. (for mobile)
